Alkastrand Beach

Alkastrand form part of the Indian ocean and it is the main beach in the northen side of KwaZulu Natal it is a great place to visit during festive season as many well known celebrities visit the beach and you can enjoy playing games like soccer and volleyball on the picin area of the beach also you can do a little shopping as the is a market where they sell swim wear and scupltures and a lot of nice things you can use to decorate your home. Near the beach the is also swimming pools and soon the is going to be a water park which will attract a lot of torists and open job opportunities for many people.

Naval Island

Naval Island is a small island near Richards Bay waterfront mostly people visit the place to chill and have pinics it is a great place to hold picnics or braai or just to get away from the city and enjoy your free time.

Richards Bay Waterfroont

The Richards Bay waterfront also known as Tuzi Gazi which is derived from two rivers which flow into the habour called uMhlathuze river and Mzingazi river has a number of restaurants one can visit depending on kind of food one is craving. You can enjoy your meal while looking at beautiful view of the yacht harbour, you can also have your meal inside a pool at Richards Bay Waterfront hotel.

Skate Park

The is a skate park right inside the beach that one can visit if they like to skate or watch people skate as the are always competiotions held. Some people use the skate park for photoshots as the is beautiful graffics in the skate park.

Boardwalk Inkwazi Mall

The shopping mall is over 65000sqm and has over 160 stores. It open from 9am till 9pm. The Boardwalk shopping mall is the right place to come to when you are in search of the culinary convenience of restaurants, the instant gratification of yummy fast-food chains or grocery shopping at famous South African favourites like Checkers, Pick 'n Pay or Woolworths stores, where you will find daily staples, clothing and more. It says a lot that Boardwalk Inkwazi is lovingly described as 'remaining not only a welcoming shopping destination but a comprehensive haven of retail mastery' in this beautiful corner of KwaZulu-Natal. This shopping mall in Richards Bay serves shoppers of all kinds in Zululand and should not be confused with the similarly named Boardwalk complex on the Port Elizabeth beachfront – further south on the coast in the Eastern Cape.

Zululand Trade Fair

The Zululand trade fair takes place every year during winter holidays the is a lot that take place during the fair. It is 3 day event and it meant for every age group. The are games for kids and also games for adults like car racing, quade bike riding. If you looking for ways to make money you can have a stall and sell what ever you want to sell as they sell everything from cars, clothes to food. During the fair the is also entertainment organised where well know artists come to perform with local artists. The is also a pegant that is held during the fair which is for crowing of Miss Zululand which is a biggest title in the area.
